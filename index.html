About

Welcome to my Osaka page. There are so many impressive points about this city that I could write pages and pages about it. However, I would like to pick just the best parts that I love and share some information with you.


Osaka is located in the western region of Japan known as the Kansai region. It is surrounded by well-known prefectures such as Kyoto, Hyogo, and Nara to name a few. Osaka is also known as Japan's Kitchen. This title means that out of all the prefectures in Japan, Osaka is the most famous for its cuisine.

Tourism

Concerning tourism, I will focus on theme parks, family oriented and natural past times that the whole family can enjoy. It is safe to say that even with things that exist in other countries, Osaka always finds a way to personalize it to a point that it becomes one of a kind in the whole world.
